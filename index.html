Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Nexus.

How does global edge deployment work?
Nexus uses a proprietary edge network with over 300 points of presence globally. When you deploy, your code and data are replicated across these nodes, ensuring users are always routed to the nearest available server.
Is Nexus compliant with enterprise security standards?
Yes, Nexus is SOC2 Type II, GDPR, and HIPAA compliant. We provide bank-grade encryption at rest and in transit, along with automated threat detection and DDoS protection.
Can I migrate my existing infrastructure to Nexus?
Absolutely. We provide automated migration tools for AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. Our engineering support team is also available to help with complex enterprise migrations.
